How to use this website/Frequency Asked Questions

This is a collaborative storytelling website. You must register to use this site. Once you do, log in. After doing so, you will be brought to a landing page (your "personal" page). From here, you choose to: When viewing the other stories on this website, you will only be shown the latest update to a story and the person that made that update. You will not be shown the whole thing. From here, you can choose to add to it yourself. There is a 50-word limit to your update.
  • View the other stories present on this website
  • Create a new story
  • Read the stories you've personally contributed to
 Note that you will not be allowed to update a story you've contributed to already. No problem, as the instant you successfully submit your update or create a story, you can access it on your landing page in its entirety.

Have fun!
